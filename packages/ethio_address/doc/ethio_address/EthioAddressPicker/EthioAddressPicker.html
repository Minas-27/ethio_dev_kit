Creates an EthioAddressPicker.

  • level controls which fields are visible. Defaults to showing all three levels.
  • initialSelection pre-populates the picker; invalid or unknown ids are ignored and the control will normalise them on init.
  • onChanged is called whenever the selection changes; it receives an EthioAddressSelection with nullable regionId, zoneId, and woredaId fields.
  • regionDecoration, zoneDecoration, and woredaDecoration allow the caller to customise the InputDecoration for each dropdown.
